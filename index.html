About Us

Kaptaan Shaab Grill House was started 4 years ago in Delhi. It was a dream project of people who came from different backgrounds but shared a similar passion. Customers loved our menu and gave us a huge success. Captain Grill originally came up with a premier quick service restaurant and later, we also started our dine-in cafe. As we know that life is better together and everyone is happy when they’re with family. We treat everyone who walks through our doors like an essential part of our family, sharing palatable food with the people we love, and spending the time together. Captain Grill provides fresh food items, hearty portions, good service, fair prices and a multi-choice menu, all these add up to good value for money for customers, and generates brand loyalty as well.
